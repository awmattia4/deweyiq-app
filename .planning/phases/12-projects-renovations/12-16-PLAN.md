**src/actions/projects-reports.ts:**

- `getProjectDashboardData(token)` — For the /projects page dashboard (PROJ-80):
  - Pipeline counts by stage (for kanban badge numbers)
  - Active projects calendar data (phases with dates for calendar overlay)
  - Crew utilization: per tech, hours allocated to projects vs route hours this week/month
  - Alerts: stalled projects, at-risk profitability, expiring permits, overdue inspections
  - Total active project value, total collected, total outstanding

- `getProjectReports(token, filters)` — Aggregate reports (PROJ-82):
  - Revenue by period: monthly project revenue (completed projects) with trends
  - Average margin by project type: replaster avg 35%, new pool avg 22%, etc.
  - Lead-to-close conversion rate (PROJ-83): leads created / proposals approved / projects completed, avg time per stage
  - Duration by type: average project duration by type
  - Subcontractor spend: total by sub, by trade, by period
  - Material cost trends: actual vs estimated across projects

**src/actions/projects-portal.ts:**
Portal-specific data fetching for customer project views. ALL queries use customer's auth context (withRls with customer token):

- `getPortalProjects(token)` — Fetch projects for the logged-in customer. Return: name, type, stage, progress %, contract amount, next milestone.
- `getPortalProjectDetail(token, projectId)` — Project detail for portal: timeline (phases with status, dates, progress), photo gallery (grouped by phase), next milestone, estimated completion. Excludes internal notes, profitability data, sub payment details.
- `getPortalProjectFinancials(token, projectId)` — PROJ-85: contract amount, paid to date, retainage held, balance due, next payment info. Does NOT expose cost breakdown or margin.
- `getPortalPunchList(token, projectId)` — PROJ-89: punch list items with status. Customer can see items and sign off when all are resolved.
- `sendProjectUpdateNotification(projectId, updateType)` — PROJ-86: configurable notifications. Update types: phase_started, phase_completed (with photos), change_order_pending, inspection_result, punch_list_ready, warranty_activated. Email with relevant details.

**Update src/lib/db/schema/portal-messages.ts:**

    6. Verify full-screen restriction overlay appears
    7. Verify /billing page is still accessible
    8. Update payment method via Stripe Portal to valid card (4242...)

    10. Verify restrictions lifted (overlay removed)
    11. Verify failed_payment_count reset to 0
  </action>
</task>

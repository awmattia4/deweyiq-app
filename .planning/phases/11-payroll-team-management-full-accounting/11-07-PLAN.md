  <name>Task 1: Create financial report actions</name>
  <files>src/actions/financial-reports.ts</files>
  <action>
    Create `src/actions/financial-reports.ts`:

    1. **`getProfitAndLoss(startDate: string, endDate: string)`** — Owner/office only. Queries journal_entry_lines joined with chart_of_accounts, filtered by entry_date range. Groups by account_type + account:
       - Income accounts: sum of line amounts (negative values = credits = revenue). Display as positive numbers.
       - Expense accounts: sum of line amounts (positive values = debits = expenses). Display as positive numbers.
       - Total Revenue, Total Expenses, Net Profit/Loss.
       - Include per-account breakdown within each category.
       - Return both simplified format (category totals with pool-friendly display names) and detailed format (all accounts with amounts).

    2. **`getBalanceSheet(asOfDate: string)`** — Owner/office only. Queries all journal_entry_lines with entry_date <= asOfDate:
       - Assets: sum of lines where account_type='asset' (positive = debits = assets)
       - Liabilities: sum of lines where account_type='liability' (negative = credits = liabilities, display as positive)
       - Equity: sum of lines where account_type='equity' (negative = credits = equity, display as positive)
       - Net Income (current period): income - expenses since last period close
       - Balance check: Assets = Liabilities + Equity + Net Income
       - Return per-account breakdown within each category.

    3. **`getCashFlowStatement(startDate: string, endDate: string)`** — Owner/office only. Derives cash flow from journal entries:
       - Operating: payments received (Cr AR), payments to vendors (Dr AP), expenses paid
       - Investing: (mostly empty for pool companies — equipment purchases if categorized)
       - Financing: owner draws/contributions (equity account changes)
       - Net cash change = Operating + Investing + Financing
       - Opening cash position (sum of asset accounts with type 'checking'|'savings' as of startDate)
       - Closing cash position = Opening + Net cash change

    4. **`getFinancialSnapshot()`** — Owner/office only. Quick summary for dashboard:
       - Current month revenue, expenses, profit
       - Cash position (sum of bank account type assets)
       - AR outstanding (sum of AR account balance)
       - AP outstanding (sum of AP account balance)
       - Month-over-month trend (compare to prior month)

    5. **`getTrialBalance(asOfDate: string)`** — Accountant mode. Lists all accounts with total debits and total credits. Debits must equal Credits (validation).

    All queries use withRls. Chart of accounts auto-seeded via ensureChartOfAccounts on first call.
